Steps to enjoy being a videographer!

  1. Complete a degree in Masscom/New Media Communication.
  2. Getting familiar with the equipment and technique.
  3. Polish your skills by starting to record any footage using your favourite songs.
  4. Look for many references from your inspirations or through social media.
  5. Join any group or communities that are related to your interest.
  6. Keep 1 notebook that you can sketch/jot down your ideas.
  7. Post your videos to social media and be open to receive critic and always do the best
